Gym Grip

Hold tight. Win right.

How to Play

  • Click an equipment in the gym.
  • Keep holding the mouse (or finger) on it until the clock hits its target time.
  • Release at or after the target to complete it. Accuracy = more points.
  • Don’t slip off the equipment – that resets your hold.
  • Finish all equipment to win. Press Esc/P to pause.
